Doggie Vacations

“Molly thought she'd died and gone to doggie heaven.”

Doggie Vacations offers a door-to-park dog walking and dog watching service that gathers dogs in an outdoor space to socialize with other dogs in the program.

Designed to appeal to dog-loving residents near Central Park in New York City, This ad evokes the style of cartoons published in The New Yorker magazine to give a fun loving feeling to viewers.
