Real NY Bike Tours

NYC Bicycle Tours

NYC-based Real NY Bike Tours is re-launching their extreme, outrageous bike tours of lower Manhattan and Brooklyn and wanted a website that shouts “crazy fun”.

Website Architecture, User Experience and Design

Craig Webb Art proposed a mobile-first responsive website with a sign-up form designed so that tourists can quickly make reservations by phone.

The quirky design is intended to attract tourists who want to explore NYC by bike

The custom WordPress theme was designed and built using SASS, JavaScript, PHP and HTML.
