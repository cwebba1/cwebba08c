About this prototype

This is a modal that opens an iframe. It is not a light-box but it will serve.

It is a little buggy. Sometimes clicking on the image results in a modal with no image, and clicking again results on the iframe displaying full-size with overflow hidden.

I started from a resource by antelove at Stack Overflow, “Open-iframe-in-bootstrap-modal-on-click”

The HandOff image on the right is a better layout. The A tag is inside the figure element.
